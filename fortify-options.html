1.0 · PURPOSE

Fortify is a six-month, one-to-one coaching engagement for senior operators. It treats the person as one interconnected system — not a collection of separate dimensions — and builds the work around the whole of who someone is, not just the parts that are easy to talk about.

The existence you always knew was possible isn't found by optimizing harder. It's found by going through the reckoning — with someone beside you — and rebuilding from what's actually true.

2.0 · SCOPE

The engagement covers ten domains of a full life — physical, mental, cognitive, sexual, relational, social, financial, environmental, spiritual, identity — organized across three layers, from biological foundation to professional expression. The domains most frameworks omit are in scope here deliberately; they carry some of the heaviest documented weight.

This is not therapy. It is for functional adults who are already well and want to perform and live more fully. Where clinical needs surface, they are referred, not improvised.

3.0 · METHOD

Align. The full domain network is mapped and a keystone identified — the highest-leverage point in the system, where targeted work cascades across everything else.

Investigate. The work goes where the map points: the places that have been managed, not addressed.

Strengthen. Identity, capacity, relationships, performance — built from the bedrock up, with physical and psychological work integrated by design.

Move forward. The engagement ends with the architecture standing and the map in the client's hands.

Foundation precedes depth throughout: without a regulated nervous system, insight doesn't stick and change doesn't sustain.
