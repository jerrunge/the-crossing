F'IT to Begin

I thought it was the final outcome of the story… I didn't know it was actually the beginning. Born out of the moment I stripped every layer off my body, slipped into the cold rushing water, and felt my entire being come alive.

I thought F'IT was it, but true to my ADHD form, it was simply the vision… the belief. Now it is time to make it real, lasting, scaffolded, strong foundation… Fortified. Just like what's happening for me, my soul.

I knew, after years of performing in every room, in every way, and sometimes thriving on the applause, that it was time to simply enter every facet of life as Jeremy Runge. A person that walks through the reckoning and does the work.

My heart is full, beating hard, and admittedly anxious as I look at the road ahead, sharing the truths and bridging the gaps on the most beautiful rebuild.

# Jeremy Runge
> Dad, coach, and writer helping senior operators rebuild themselves without losing what made them effective.

Jeremy Runge is a leadership coach and writer based in Pacifica, California. He works with senior operators — executives, founders, and high-performers — navigating the harder reckoning that comes after years of running fast: what was lost, what was built, and what is still left to become. He coaches through Fortify™, a bespoke six-month 1:1 engagement built on a 10-domain framework grounded in 183 randomized controlled trials. He writes about leadership, reinvention, and the work of being — not from the other side of it, but from inside it.

## Themes
A: The Reckoning — the professional mid-reckoning that high-performers don't have language for.
B: The Rebuild — structural reinvention of self, not just career pivots. All ten domains as a system.
C: Real Leadership — leadership as a way of being. Empathy as force multiplier.

## Career
Two decades operating at scale: Apple (Director, Global Retail Sales & People Operations — 550+ stores, 25+ countries, 60,000+ team members), GoPuff/BevMo (VP Retail, $900M+ P&L, decline reversed to growth), CarMax, Target. Now Founder & Principal, Reck the Fort LLC — fractional and advisory operating work plus Fortify™ coaching.
